Blog 38: Vitamin D and Fertility: A Hidden Factor

Fertility challenges can be complex, but nutrition is one area that's often overlooked. Research increasingly supports Vitamin D's role in reproductive health for both men and women.

How It Helps

Vitamin D helps regulate hormone production, ovarian follicle development, and endometrial lining preparation in women. In men, it's linked to sperm quality and testosterone levels.

Fertility Benefits

  • Supports menstrual regularity and ovulation
  • Enhances implantation readiness
  • May improve sperm motility and count
