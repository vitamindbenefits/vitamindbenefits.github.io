Blog 37: Vitamin D and Cardiovascular Health: A Vital Connection

Heart disease remains the leading cause of death globally. Vitamin D has emerged as a key nutrient with cardiovascular benefits.

Heart-Healthy Benefits

Vitamin D helps regulate blood pressure, supports endothelial function (the inner lining of blood vessels), and may reduce arterial stiffness.

Low Vitamin D levels have been associated with a higher risk of hypertension, stroke, and heart attacks.

Strengthen Your Heart

  • Supports healthy blood pressure
  • Aids in calcium regulation in arteries
