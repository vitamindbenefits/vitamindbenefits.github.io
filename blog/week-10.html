Foundational Support for Two

Vitamin D supports calcium metabolism, immune function, and cellular growth. It aids in fetal bone development and has been shown to reduce the risk of complications such as gestational diabetes, low birth weight, and preterm labor.

Adequate Vitamin D is also important postpartum, helping to support mood, recovery, and breast milk nutrient quality.

Better Outcomes for Mother and Child

  • Strengthens fetal bone and teeth development
  • Supports immune system development
  • May reduce risk of gestational hypertension

Many healthcare professionals now test and monitor Vitamin D during pregnancy as part of routine prenatal care.
