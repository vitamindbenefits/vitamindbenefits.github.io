The Challenge with Traditional Vitamin D Supplements

Most vitamin D supplements come in hard tablets or capsules that must be broken down by your digestive system before nutrients can be absorbed. This process can take hours and may result in reduced bioavailability. Many people take vitamin D daily yet still struggle to maintain optimal levels, which may be partly due to delivery format challenges.
