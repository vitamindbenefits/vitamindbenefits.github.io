Blog 25: Vitamin D for Kids: Early Health Foundations

Healthy habits start early, and that includes nutritional supplementation. Vitamin D is essential for children's growth, bone development, immune strength, and even mood.

Why It Matters for Children

Children's bones grow rapidly and require Vitamin D to absorb calcium efficiently. It also supports immune development and has been linked to mood regulation in youth.

Deficiency in kids has been associated with:

  • Rickets and delayed growth
  • Weakened immune function
  • Fatigue and irritability
